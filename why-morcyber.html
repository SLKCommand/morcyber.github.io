What Makes Us Different

We Actually Understand Your Business

We don't show up with a generic checklist. We take time to learn how your business operates, what systems you use, and what you're actually worried about. Then we build a security program that makes sense for your situation — not someone else's template.

We Don't Disappear After the Assessment

Most consultants sell you an assessment, give you a 50-page report, then vanish. We stick around. We help you prioritize, implement fixes, train your team, and maintain compliance over time. We're a partner, not a vendor.

We Speak Plain English

No jargon. No acronyms (unless we explain them). We explain cybersecurity and compliance in terms business owners understand. You'll know exactly what's at risk, what it costs to fix, and why it matters.

We Give You Honest Pricing Upfront

No "it depends" or surprise bills. We tell you what things cost before you commit. You'll know the price for assessment, program build, and ongoing management from day one. No games.

We Specialize in Regulated Industries

Healthcare, financial services, tech — these industries have specific compliance requirements. We know HIPAA, FTC Safeguards, SOC 2 inside and out. We've built these programs dozens of times. You're not our guinea pig.

We Don't Oversell

If you don't need something, we'll tell you. If a $2,500 assessment solves your problem, we won't try to sell you a $40,000 program. We care more about long-term relationships than one-time sales.
